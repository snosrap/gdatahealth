Thanks, your GData Health account was created.

You can start interacting with your health profiles using the command line.

$ AUTH=`curl --data "Email=$EMAIL&Passwd=$PASSWORD&service=health" "https://gdatahealth.appspot.com/accounts/ClientLogin" | grep Auth | cut -d'=' -f2`
$ curl --header "Authorization: auth=$AUTH" "https://gdatahealth.appspot.com/health/feeds/profile/list"

Most of the functionality described at the Google Health API documentation page will work with GData Health.
